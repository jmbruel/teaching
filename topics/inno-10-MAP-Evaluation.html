2. Assignement 1 (10%)

2.1. Topic

I asked you to read a 15 pages report detailing what a good User Story is. You will provide a one page max PDF file with your answers to the final 2 questions.

2.2. Evaluation

Upload your answers on Moodle: https://moodle.university.innopolis.ru/mod/assign/view.php?id=5074

3. Assignement 2 (10%)

3.1. Topic

  • Find a provocative statement about Agile/Scrum (e.g., this one)

  • Explain in one slide/one page

  • Give a critical and argumented opinion (right/wrong)

3.2. Evaluation

Upload your answers on Moodle: https://moodle.university.innopolis.ru/mod/assign/view.php?id=5820

4. Project Report (60%)

4.1. Topic

Each of you is working on a project where you practice Scrum.

  • Context, environment

  • Product description

  • Team organization

  • Sprint plannings

  • Financial evaluation (if you had to send me a bill…​)

  • Personnal Feedback on the experience

  • …​

4.2. Evaluation

This is a group evaluation, hence make sure to put the list of all the members (registered students) of the team on your report.

You will have to put a PDF report and any other materials you believe usefull in this Moodle report:

https://moodle.university.innopolis.ru/mod/assign/view.php?id=5819

The evaluation of your report will focus on the following criterias:

Feel free to add a "self-evaluation" section in your report that self-evaluate those criteria for your project.

5. Exam (20%)

5.1. Topic

This will take the form of a one hour exam on a quizz with questions taken from the course (about Agile, Scrum, Tests, Build, Continuous Integration, etc.).

5.2. Evaluation

The quizz will have negative points for bad answers. Make sure you answer questions you’re sure about the answer.

6. Bonus (extra 10%)

6.1. Topic

For those of you who really want a more detailed, critical view on Agile methods, I advised you to follow this online MOOC: https://www.edx.org/course/agile-software-development-ethx-asd-1x

It is a good way of staying in the field between two venues and to prepare the exam.

6.2. Evaluation

Proof of success (>60%) should be sent to me by email. It will count as a bonus in the evaluation.
