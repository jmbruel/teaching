== Human aspects

[quote]
Advice on how to manage your supervisor?

[NOTE]
====
[.incremental]
- Don't wait to raise issues
- DRY (**D**on't **R**epeat **Y**our same mistakes again)
- Rephrase (make sure you get her right)
- Get him involved in your work (collaborative work)
====
